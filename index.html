Project Objective

The coming decades/centuries certainly hold some difficulties of varying magnitude and dynamics, certainly one of the biggest: Climate Change. And while impacts are becoming more and more noticeable, mitigation measures are creeping behind. To highlight the relevance of the problem, in this project we compare climate change and crop yields on a global scale. The aim is to identify which countries and crops which in particular suffer from the effects of change, such that adjustments in location, production and trade can be considered, that pursue the goal to generate enough (at best healthy) food for all humans.
